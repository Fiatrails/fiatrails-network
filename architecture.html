Design Principles

  • Non-Custodial

    FiatRails coordinates settlement instructions but never holds customer funds. All regulated activities remain with licensed institutional partners.

  • Corridor Isolation

    Each corridor operates independently with its own settlement windows, participant lists, and compliance rules. Failures in one corridor do not propagate.

  • Deterministic Execution

    FX rates are determined at window close, not executed in market. Oracle prices are locked, ensuring no slippage or front-running possibilities.

  • Technology Service Provider

    FiatRails is a non-custodial coordination protocol — not a bank, PSP, E-money issuer, or remittance company. We never hold fiat, never custody user funds, and never execute settlement. All regulated activities are performed exclusively by licensed partner institutions. FiatRails operates as technology infrastructure only.
